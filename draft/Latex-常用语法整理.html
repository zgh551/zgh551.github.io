数组形式

\[ \left \{ \begin{array}{cl} &\dfrac{dx}{df}= \dfrac{dx}{dt}\dfrac{dt}{df} = \dfrac{\dot{x}(t)}{\dot{f}(t)}\\ &\dfrac{dy}{df}= \dfrac{dy}{dt}\dfrac{dt}{df} = \dfrac{\dot{y}(t)}{\dot{f}(t)}\\ &\dfrac{d\psi}{df}= \dfrac{d\psi}{dt}\dfrac{dt}{df} = \dfrac{\dot{\psi}(t)}{\dot{f}(t)} \end{array} \right. \]
